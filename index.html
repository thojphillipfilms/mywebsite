About Me

I'm Phillip Thao, a University of Wisconsin–Madison graduate majoring in Communication Arts: TV-Radio-Film and Information Science. I am a filmmaker at heart, as well as a colorist, video editor, and content creator.

I’m passionate about capturing stories through emotion, atmosphere, and visual storytelling. I enjoy narrative work, but that doesn’t stop my creativity in brand and product work either. I love being on set, working in teams/crews, and have gained a passion to work in that enviroment.

My work focuses on creating experiences that stay with people long after the frame ends, from short films to color grading, and editing.

My Short Film

Short Film Trailer

"What it Takes" was made during my time at UW-Madison, it was a script I submitted with my co-writer and was chosen to be made for the capstone course. With a crew of 9 including me, I was AC and Colorist. The making of this short has taught me alot, from focusing, working with a crew and learning different departments.
