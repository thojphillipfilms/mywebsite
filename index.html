About Me

I'm Phillip Thao, a University of Wisconsin–Madison graduate majoring in Communication Arts: TV-Radio-Film and Information Science. I am a filmmaker at heart, as well as a colorist, video editor, and content creator.

I’m passionate about capturing stories through emotion, atmosphere, and visual storytelling. I enjoy narrative work, but that doesn’t stop my creativity in brand and product work either.

My work focuses on creating cinematic experiences that stay with people long after the frame ends — from short films to color grading and photography.
