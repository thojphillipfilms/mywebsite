About Me

I'm Phillip Thao, an University of Madison Wisconsin graduate majored in Communication Arts:Tv-Radio-Film and Information Science. I am a filmmaker at heart, also a colorist, video editor, and content creator. I am passionate about capturing stories through emotion, atmosphere, and visual storytelling. I enjoy narritive work but that doesn't stop my creativity in brand/product work either.

My work focuses on creating cinematic experiences that stay with people long after the frame ends — from creating short films to color grading and photography. I know my work will impress the audience, and help the team out.
